Dice Addition

Add up the dice as fast as you can before time runs out. Pick a difficulty below to begin your run.

How to Play

Look at the dice, add their total, type your answer, and press Enter or tap the check button.

Points

Correct answers give points, wrong answers remove points, and combo streaks can earn bonus score.

Controls

Use your keyboard to type the answer. Press Enter to submit quickly during fast rounds.

Difficulty

Easy uses 2 dice. Hard uses 3 dice. Choose one to start the countdown right away.
