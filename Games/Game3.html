MATHIVE • GAME INTRO

Factor Smash

Find two numbers that multiply to the target as fast as you can before time runs out.

+15 correct • −10 wrong • +10 if solved in 3s • +25 every 5 correct

How to Play

Look at the target number, then choose two numbers from the grid that multiply to make it.

Points

Correct answers give points, fast answers give bonus points, and every 5 correct answers gives a combo reward.

Controls

Click numbers to fill Pick 1 and Pick 2. Click a selected number again, or click a pick slot, to clear it.

Difficulty

Easy gives smaller factor pairs. Hard uses a wider range of numbers and larger products.
